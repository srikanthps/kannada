# What You Will Learn

In this chapter, you will learn common Kannada words grouped by topic.

These are words that you are likely to encounter in:

- Daily conversations
- Schools
- Shops
- Homes
- Signboards
- Newspapers

The goal is recognition rather than memorization.

# Practice Exercise

Read the following words aloud.

```
ಅಮ್ಮ

ಮನೆ

ಪುಸ್ತಕ

ಮರ

ಹೂವು

ಆನೆ

ಅನ್ನ

ಕೆಂಪು

ಸೋಮವಾರ

ನಮಸ್ಕಾರ
```

Repeat until you can read them smoothly without looking at the transliteration.

---

# Vocabulary Building Tips

- Learn five new words each day.
- Read them aloud.
- Write them once or twice.
- Try to use them in simple sentences.
- Review previously learned words regularly.

Small daily practice leads to long-term retention.

---

# Did You Know?

Many Kannada words are used every day on shop signs, bus boards, school notices, and product labels. The more vocabulary you recognize, the less often you'll need to rely on transliteration or translation.

---

# Teacher's Note

Don't try to memorize this chapter in one sitting.

Think of it as your personal vocabulary bank.

Return to it whenever you encounter a familiar word in real life.

With repeated exposure, these words will become part of your active reading vocabulary.
