# What You Will Learn

In this chapter, you will learn to recognize Kannada as it appears in everyday life.

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to read common:

- Shop signs
- Road signs
- School signboards
- Bus destination boards
- Public notices
- Product labels

The goal is recognition rather than memorization.

---

# Shop Signs

You will often see these words while walking through towns and cities in Karnataka.

| Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| ಹೋಟೆಲ್ | hotel | Hotel / Restaurant |
| ಬೇಕರಿ | bekari | Bakery |
| ಮೆಡಿಕಲ್ | medical | Medical Store |
| ಪುಸ್ತಕ ಮಳಿಗೆ | pustaka maḷige | Bookstore |
| ಹಣ್ಣು ಅಂಗಡಿ | hannu angaḍi | Fruit Shop |
| ತರಕಾರಿ ಅಂಗಡಿ | tarakaari angaḍi | Vegetable Shop |
| ಹಾಲು | haalu | Milk |
| ಚಹಾ | chahaa | Tea |

Read each sign aloud.

# Bus Destination Boards

Try reading these place names.

```
ಬೆಂಗಳೂರು

ಮೈಸೂರು

ಮಂಡ್ಯ

ತುಮಕೂರು

ಶಿವಮೊಗ್ಗ

ಧಾರವಾಡ
```

Even if you have never visited these places, practising them will improve your reading speed.

# Reading Practice

Read the following words without looking at the English meanings.

```
ಹೋಟೆಲ್

ಪ್ರವೇಶ

ನಿರ್ಗಮನ

ಶಾಲೆ

ಗ್ರಂಥಾಲಯ

ಹಣ್ಣು ಅಂಗಡಿ

ಸಕ್ಕರೆ

ನೀರು
```

Repeat until you can recognize them instantly.

---

# Real-World Practice

The next time you go outside, try reading:

- One shop sign.
- One road sign.
- One bus destination.
- One product label.
- One public notice.

You do not need to understand every word.

Simply recognizing the Kannada script is valuable practice.

---

# Common Mistakes

## Translating Every Word

When reading signs, first focus on recognizing the word.

Understanding the meaning becomes easier with repeated exposure.

---

## Ignoring Kannada Around You

Many learners practise only from books.

The best reading practice often comes from everyday life.

Read signs, labels, calendars, receipts, and notices whenever you have the opportunity.

---

# Challenge

Can you read all of the following without using the transliteration?

```
ಹೋಟೆಲ್

ಬೇಕರಿ

ನಿಲ್ಲಿ

ಪ್ರವೇಶ

ಶಾಲೆ

ಮೈಸೂರು

ಅಕ್ಕಿ

ಸಕ್ಕರೆ
```

If yes, you're well on your way to reading Kannada confidently.

---

# Did You Know?

Even spending five minutes a day reading Kannada signs and labels can expose you to hundreds of words every week. This kind of incidental reading is one of the fastest ways to build reading fluency.
