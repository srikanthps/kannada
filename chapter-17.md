# What You Will Learn

In this chapter, you will become familiar with common Kannada terms used in:

- Primary schools
- Kannada textbooks
- Classroom instructions
- Homework
- Examinations

Understanding these words will help you follow lessons and use Kannada learning materials with confidence.

---

# Parts of the Kannada Language

| Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| ವರ್ಣಮಾಲೆ | varṇamāle | Alphabet |
| ಸ್ವರ | swara | Vowel |
| ವ್ಯಂಜನ | vyanjana | Consonant |
| ಅಕ್ಷರ | akshara | Letter / Syllable |
| ಮಾತ್ರೆ | mātre | Vowel sign |
| ಕಾಗುಣಿತ | kāguṇita | Consonant–vowel combinations |
| ಒತ್ತಕ್ಷರ | ottakshara | Conjunct consonant |
| ಅರ್ಕಾವತ್ತು | arkāvattu | Special form of ರ್ before another consonant |

Read the following words aloud.


# Reading Challenge

Read the following classroom instructions without using the transliteration.

```text
ಓದಿ

ಬರೆಯಿರಿ

ಕೇಳಿ

ಹೇಳಿ

ನೋಡಿ

ಉತ್ತರಿಸಿ

ಗಮನಿಸಿ
```

Can you understand what each instruction means?

# Tips for Learners

Whenever you open a Kannada textbook:

- Read the chapter title.
- Look for familiar classroom words.
- Try to understand the instructions before reading the lesson.
- Keep this chapter bookmarked for quick reference.

Over time, these terms will become second nature.

---

# Did You Know?

Many Kannada textbooks use the same classroom vocabulary from primary school through high school. Learning these words once will help you understand educational materials for many years.

---

# Teacher's Note

Don't be discouraged if some educational terms feel unfamiliar.

You don't need to memorize every word immediately.

As you continue reading Kannada books, you'll encounter these terms repeatedly, and they'll soon become part of your everyday vocabulary.
